PPC Wizzard

PPC Wizard fully automates all advertising of your products!

This revolutionary tool is suitable for both beginners and professionals.

Here's what it does:

  1. Creates an automatic high-bid campaign that collects keywords and statistics for research campaigns. After 4 weeks, it decreases bids.

  2. Finds the most relevant keywords and transfer them to the Research campaign in different match types.

  3. Tests the received keywords in the Research campaign and then transfers the successful ones to the Main campaig

  4. Turns on the Autopilot in the Main campaign, which adjusts the bids, finds new search queries and tests them in different match types, and also gets rid of inefficient search queries and keywords.

You can view the history of the actions and statistics of the Wizard, and also turn it on and off at any time.
